# Clinical Clarity

This image should feel calm, credible, and carefully composed, with the visual weight of a healthcare education brand rather than a generic stock banner. The composition needs to read instantly on a website card, so the hierarchy should be bold but quiet, with one clear focal point and minimal distraction.

Color should stay within a soft lilac, white, navy, and teal family so it feels native to the current site while still looking refined. Any accent shapes should be restrained and purposeful, like small signals of learning, guidance, and trust. The mood is supportive, not promotional.

The image should use rounded geometry, gentle shadows, and clean spacing to create a sense of order and reassurance. It should imply OET preparation through visual cues such as notes, checkmarks, speech, and pathway motifs, but the work should remain elegant and not literal or cluttered.

The final image must look meticulously crafted, as if it were refined over many passes by someone with strong visual judgment. Every edge, contrast choice, and spacing decision should feel deliberate, polished, and master-level.
