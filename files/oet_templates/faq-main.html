Frequently Asked Questions

Answers for nurses, doctors, and pharmacists

What is included in each OET package?

Every package includes scheduled speaking and writing practice, detailed feedback, and UK pathway-focused strategy sessions.

How are classes delivered?

Coaching is delivered online with live one-to-one sessions, recorded support materials, and action plans.

Can I join if I have zero OET experience?

Yes. We start by assessing your current level and build a roadmap for both your strengths and urgent gaps.

Do you support all OET sub-skills?

We cover speaking and writing first, with supportive guidance in listening and reading strategy as needed.

Where do you coach from?

We support learners worldwide with sessions and support channels designed for UK pathway preparation.
